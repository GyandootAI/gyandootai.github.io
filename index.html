Frequently asked questions

Honest answers to common questions

Is the paper really generated in 60 seconds?

+
Yes. Once you submit the form, our system sends your requirements to the AI and delivers two Google Docs to your email — typically within 1 to 3 minutes. The 60 seconds refers to the AI generation time itself. If your email is slow, it may take a few minutes longer.

How accurate is the board format?

+
Very accurate. The AI is trained on CBSE, ICSE, and major state board question paper patterns. It follows the correct section structure, question types, and mark distribution for each board. That said, we always recommend reviewing the paper before printing — the teacher knows their class best.

What happens after my 3 free papers?

+
You will see an upgrade prompt. You can WhatsApp or email us to upgrade to any paid plan. We will set you up within minutes. No automatic charges — you only pay when you choose to upgrade.

Can I edit the generated paper?

+
Absolutely. Every paper arrives as a Google Doc which you have full edit access to. Change questions, adjust marks, add your school name and logo, or rearrange sections. Download as PDF or Word when ready. The paper is yours.

Which subjects and boards are supported?

+
CBSE, ICSE, and major state boards (Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Rajasthan, West Bengal, Kerala, Himachal Pradesh) for Classes 6 to 12. All Science, Commerce, and Arts subjects. UPSC General Studies also supported.

Is my data safe? Who sees my request?

+
Your name, email, and subject request are stored securely in our database. We never share your information with anyone. The paper is sent only to your email. We do not store the generated papers.

How do I pay after the free trial?

+
WhatsApp us at the number shown on the upgrade screen, or email [email protected]. We send you a secure Razorpay payment link. Takes 2 minutes. We activate your account immediately after payment.
