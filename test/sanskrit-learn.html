What does "Yoga" actually mean in Sanskrit?
Beautiful question! योग (Yoga) comes from the root युज् (yuj) meaning "to yoke, join, or unite." It shares its root with the English word "yoke."

Patanjali defines it in Sutra 1.2: योगश्चित्तवृत्तिनिरोधः — "Yoga is the cessation of the fluctuations of the mind." The word itself points to union — of individual consciousness with universal consciousness. Would you like to break down that sutra word by word?
